About Me

I'm Josh O'Connor. I'm currently working at Magic Leap, helping define mixed reality gaming.

I've been making games since 2010. I started at Sidhe Interactive in New Zealand working on a console title, then the studio became PikPok when it shifted focus over to mobile. I worked my way up to a senior programmer position and was even lead programmer on a few titles. I made the shift back into console games at Mediatonic.
